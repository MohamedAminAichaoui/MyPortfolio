Why did I choose IIT?

I chose the International Institute of Technology (IIT) because it is a private higher education institution accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, offering a unique educational model inspired by North American systems.

Founded in 2012, IIT stands out as the first and only institution in southern Tunisia to achieve the prestigious **EUR-ACE速** and **EURO-INF速** labels for its engineering programs, ensuring international recognition and excellence.

Beyond academics, IIT fosters an environment of innovation and entrepreneurship, providing practical training aligned with market demands and hosting over 1,000 international students, which creates a truly global learning atmosphere.
